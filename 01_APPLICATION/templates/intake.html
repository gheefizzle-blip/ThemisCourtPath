9 Child Support Worksheet

This is the Arizona Parent's Worksheet for Child Support (DRS12F). These values will be filled onto pages 15-17 of the petition packet. The worksheet calculates the actual child support obligation based on both parents' incomes.

Tip: The "Father" and "Mother" columns on the worksheet correspond to the parents' roles, not petitioner/respondent. The system will map them correctly based on the relationship info you entered in Step 3.
Auto-calculation: Fields highlighted in gold are calculated automatically as you type. You can still override any value manually if needed. Only the Basic Child Support Obligation (Line 21) must be looked up from the AZ Guidelines table — it cannot be calculated from the inputs.

Parenting Time Schedule

The Arizona Guidelines calculate a parenting time adjustment based on the exact number of overnights per year each parent has with the child. Build the schedule below or enter the totals directly at the bottom.

How overnights work: An "overnight" means the child sleeps at that parent's home. The total for both parents must equal 365 days/year. The non-custodial parent's day count determines whether Table A (<110 days) or Table B (110+ days) is used.

12 Court Filing Fee Information

After your documents are prepared, you will file them with the court.

The court charges a filing fee, typically $200 - $350 depending on your county. This fee is separate from the $99 you pay Themis Court Path and is paid directly to the court.

If you cannot afford this fee, Arizona allows you to apply for a:

  • Fee deferral (pay later, on a schedule)
  • Fee waiver (do not pay at all)

The court will decide whether you qualify.

Important: Themis Court Path does not determine whether you qualify for a fee waiver. The court will make that decision based on your application. Your selection here is for your own records and does not affect the $99 Themis Court Path fee.
