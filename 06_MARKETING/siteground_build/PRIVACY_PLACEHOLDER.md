## Short Summary (allowed pre-publication)

Until the formal policy is published, the following short summary describes how we handle data. This summary is not a substitute for the formal policy.

- We collect only what the court forms require (names, dates of birth, addresses, employment, income, and similar facts about the parties and child).
- We encrypt your intake data before it is stored.
- We never see or store your payment card information; Stripe handles that directly.
- We do not sell your data, share it with marketers, or use it to train AI models.
- The only third party we share court-relevant data with is the court system you choose to file with, and only when you authorize the filing.
