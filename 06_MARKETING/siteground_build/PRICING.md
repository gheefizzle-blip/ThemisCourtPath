Included in the $99:
- Guided 11-step intake wizard

- Parenting Time Schedule Builder

- Court-ready filled PDF
- Validation report of every filled field

Not included in the $99:
- Court filing fee. The court charges its own separate filing fee, typically $200 to $350 depending on county. You pay this directly to the court when you file.
- Process server fee. If you need someone to formally serve the other parent, certified process servers in Arizona typically charge $80 to $175. You hire and pay them separately.
- Attorney consultation. If you want a licensed Arizona attorney to review your documents, that is a separate service we may offer in the future.
