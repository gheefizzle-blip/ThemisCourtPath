## How Themis Court Path Works

### 11-Step Guided Intake Wizard
No legal jargon. No confusing forms. Just clear questions in plain English.

1. **Your Information** — Name, address, contact details
2. **Respondent Information** — The other parent's details
3. **Jurisdiction** — Why you're filing in this county
4. **Relationship & Paternity** — How parentage is established
5. **Child Support Details** — Who pays, insurance, medical expenses
6. **Child Information** — Children's names, DOBs, living arrangements
7. **Legal Disclosures** — Required court questions (DCS cases, other cases)
8. **Employment** — Both parents' employer information
9. **Parenting Time** — Custody schedule builder
10. **Income & Worksheet** — Monthly income and support calculations
11. **Review & Generate** — Final check before document creation

### Arizona Child Support Calculator
The Arizona Guidelines require looking up values in the state Schedule based on combined income and number of children. Our calculator:
- Covers $950 to $30,000 combined monthly income
- Supports 1-6 children
- Interpolates between data points for precise amounts
- Uses the 2022 Guidelines revision (effective January 2023)
- Automatically selects Table A or Table B based on parenting time

### Parenting Time Schedule Builder
Building a custody schedule is one of the most complex parts of a child support filing. Our visual builder lets you:
- Set weeknight and weekend schedules
- Allocate holidays and school breaks
- Define summer arrangements
- Auto-calculate total overnights for each parent
- Determine Table A (<110 days) vs Table B (110+ days) automatically
- Override with direct day-count entry if you already know your numbers

### Complete PDF Generation
Themis fills every field on the official 29-page Navajo County petition packet:
- Personal information on all pages
- Sensitive Data Sheet (page 4-5)
- Family Court Cover Sheet (page 6-8)
- Petition to Establish Child Support (pages 9-13)
- Parent's Worksheet for Child Support (pages 15-17)
- Order to Appear (pages 18-19)
- Child Support Order (pages 20-28)
- Employer Information Sheet (page 29)

Plus checkboxes, radio buttons, and the worksheet overlay — all automated.
