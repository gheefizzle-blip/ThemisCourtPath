## Why We Built Themis

Every year, over 50,000 child support cases are filed in Arizona. Many of those families can't afford an attorney. They're left to navigate a 29-page court petition on their own — decoding legal jargon, looking up guidelines tables, calculating worksheet values, and hoping they checked the right boxes.

We saw this problem and knew software could solve it. The same technology that helps engineers manage billion-dollar infrastructure can guide a parent through a court form.

Themis Court Path doesn't replace attorneys — it makes the paperwork accessible so people can focus on what matters: their families.
