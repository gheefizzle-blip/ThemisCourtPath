## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's NOT included in the price?**
Court filing fees (varies by county, typically $200-350). Process server fees if needed ($80-175). These are paid directly to the court and server, not to us.

**Can I get a refund?**
Before your documents are generated: full refund. After generation: no refund, as the service has been completed. If there's a software error, we'll fix it or refund — your choice.

**Do I need the Attorney Consultation?**
Not required. But if you have questions about jurisdiction, paternity, or complex custody situations, 20 minutes with an attorney can save you from costly mistakes.

**What about filing fees?**
Court filing fees are separate and paid directly to the court. If you can't afford the filing fee, Arizona courts offer fee waivers and payment plans — we'll explain how to apply.
